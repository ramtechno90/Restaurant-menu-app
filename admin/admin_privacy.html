Admin Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 25, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of authorized administrators ("Admin," "you") who use the Admin Page and all of its features ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information from Admins:

  • Email Address: Used as a unique identifier for your account and for login purposes.
  • Password: A hashed version of your password is stored for account authentication and security. We never store plaintext passwords.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email address and password are used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and grant you access to the Service.
  • To secure your account and the data associated with your restaurant.

We do not use your email address for marketing or promotional communications.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account information is stored securely in our database, hosted by Supabase. We utilize Supabase's built-in authentication and security features, including password hashing, to protect your credentials. Access to this information is strictly limited.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal admin information to outside parties. This information is confidential and used solely for the operation of the Service.

6. Your Control Over Your Information

If you need to update your email address or password, please contact the service provider for assistance.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this Admin Privacy Policy, please contact the service provider directly.
